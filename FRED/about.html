Ruptures in Sri Lanka's Education:

Ever since the kannangara reforms and Sri Lanka's independence, our education system has been gradually falling apart due to weak education reforms. Despite having a free education system, it is not necessarily free as majority of the students spand a fortune on tuition classes. Those that have access to good tuition classes often end up going to state universities while others have no other option but to spend even more resources on private universities.
